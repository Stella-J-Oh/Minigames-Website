Hangman


Instructions:

Press play to start the game. You have 6 lives. Enter a single letter in the guessing field and CLICK the button "Guess." Do NOT press the Enter key when submitting your guess. If you enter a letter in the word, you do not lose a life and are allowed to continue guessing. If you enter a letter that is not in the word, you will lose 1 life. Keep guessing letters until you guess the word or run out of lives. No humans were harmed in the process of coding this game
